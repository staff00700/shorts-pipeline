Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Shorts Uploader ("we", "our", "the app") is an automation tool that uploads short-form video content to social media platforms on behalf of users. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

To provide our service, we collect and process the following data:

  • YouTube account information: channel name, basic channel metadata (accessed via OAuth 2.0)
  • Video content: video files that you authorize us to upload
  • OAuth tokens: access and refresh tokens required to post content on your behalf

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Uploading short-form video content to your connected social media accounts
  • Managing video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags)
  • Maintaining authentication sessions to keep the service running

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored encrypted on our server. Video files are processed temporarily and deleted after upload. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your video content and account information are only used to perform the upload operations you authorize.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube (Google API): for uploading video content to YouTube
  • TikTok (TikTok API): for uploading video content to TikTok

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data they process.

7. User Rights

You may revoke our app's access at any time through your Google Account permissions page or TikTok authorized apps settings. Upon revocation, we will cease all operations on your behalf.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email associated with your GitHub account.
